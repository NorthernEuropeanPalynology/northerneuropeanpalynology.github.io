NEPal - Nothern European Palynology Database

Our aim is to collaborate on sharing pollen material that will potentially fascilitate the methodological development of automated systems used in palynology. Pollen analysis is a tool applied broadly in different research fields such as paleoecology, areobiology, pollinator-plant interactions, forensic palynology and melissopalynology. However, due to the extend of expertise and time required to analyze diverse pollen samples, automatization was seen as a possible solution relatively early.

This collaboration to date consists of researcher and students from the respective fields mentioned above.
