The Global Pollen Project was founded with the aim of creating a centralized database of pollen images and data from across the globe. Founded by the University of Oxford, the project emphasizes open access and collaboration among scientists worldwide. The project's impact is evident in its growing database, increased research collaborations, and the adoption of its tools and resources by the scientific community. Visit their official website for more detailed information and current statistics.
